Welcome to Greece!

          Greece has always been one of my dream destinations! There’s something truly magical about its rich history, stunning architecture, and breathtaking landscapes. Walking through the ancient ruins of Athens would feel like traveling back in time, while the white-washed buildings of Santorini offer a picture-perfect escape.

          And then there’s the food! I can’t wait to indulge in authentic gyros, fresh seafood straight from the Mediterranean, and golden, flaky baklava dripping with honey. Whether it’s exploring historical sites, sailing across the Aegean Sea, or immersing myself in Greek traditions, I know Greece will be an unforgettable adventure!

TRAVEL

          Getting to Greece from Manila is pretty simple, but it does take a bit of planning. Since there aren’t any direct flights, the best way is to book a flight with one or two layovers, usually in Doha, Dubai, Istanbul, or Singapore. The journey typically takes around 16 to 24 hours, depending on the airline and layover time.

LANDMARKS

          There are so many incredible places to visit in Greece, but the ones I’m most excited about are the Acropolis of Athens, Santorini, and the Parthenon.

          First, the Acropolis of Athens is a must-see. It’s one of the most famous ancient sites in the world, and I can’t wait to walk through its ruins, imagining what life was like back in ancient Greece. The Parthenon, which stands at the heart of the Acropolis, is another spot I’m excited for. It’s a masterpiece of architecture, and seeing it in person would be like stepping into history.

          Then, there’s Santorini, a place I’ve always dreamed of visiting. The iconic white-washed buildings, blue-domed churches, and stunning sunsets make it one of the most beautiful places in the world. I’d love to explore its charming streets, relax by the sea, and just take in the breathtaking views. Greece is full of history, beauty, and unforgettable experiences, and I can’t wait to visit these incredible places!

EXPERIENCE

          Greece isn’t just about stunning views and ancient ruins. It’s also a country full of rich traditions, delicious food, and vibrant culture. One of the things I’d love to experience is Easter in Greece, which is one of their biggest celebrations. Imagine grand feasts, fireworks, and age-old traditions bringing the whole country to life. Of course, I can’t visit Greece without watching a Greek dance performance. The energy of traditional dances like sirtaki is something I’d love to see in person and maybe even try myself. When it comes to food, I already know I’ll be obsessed with baklava, a sweet flaky pastry soaked in honey and stuffed with nuts. And for a fun treat, I’d love to try loukoumades, crispy little dough balls drizzled with honey, almost like Greek doughnuts. Greece is all about flavor, tradition, and unforgettable experiences, and I can’t wait to immerse myself in it all.
