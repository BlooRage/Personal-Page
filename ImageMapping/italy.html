Welcome to Italy!

I have always dreamed of going to Europe and hopefully working there in the future. Italy is definitely on my list. I want to visit because of its rich history and stunning architecture. From the Colosseum in Rome to the beautiful canals of Venice, every place feels like a masterpiece. And of course, I can’t forget about the world-famous cuisine. I would love to try authentic pasta and pizza, sip on rich, flavorful wine, and enjoy the breathtaking landscapes of the Amalfi Coast and Tuscany. Italy is a place full of beauty, culture, and amazing experiences, and I can’t wait to explore it someday!

TRAVEL

If you’re traveling to Italy from Manila, the first thing you need is a Schengen visa, since Italy is part of the Schengen Area. Make sure to prepare your passport, flight itinerary, hotel bookings, and proof of financial capacity. Once that’s sorted, you can book a flight from NAIA to major Italian cities like Rome, Milan, or Venice. There are no direct flights, so expect a layover in places like Dubai, Doha, or Istanbul, with a total travel time of around 15 to 20 hours.

LANDMARKS

Italy is home to some of the world’s most breathtaking architectural wonders, and I can't wait to experience them! First on my list is the Leaning Tower of Pisa, where I have to take the classic tourist photo pretending to "hold it up." Then, there’s the Venice Grand Canal, where I can picture myself riding a gondola, gliding past historic buildings, and soaking in the city's romantic charm. And of course, I can’t miss the Colosseum in Rome—just imagining the gladiator battles and the incredible history behind this massive amphitheater gives me chills. From ancient ruins to stunning waterways, Italy’s architecture feels like stepping into a time machine, and I can't wait to see it all!

EXPERIENCE

         Italy is a country bursting with flavor, culture, and unforgettable traditions, and I can't wait to experience it all firsthand! First, the food! I’m most excited to try authentic Neapolitan pizza in Naples—the birthplace of pizza itself. Just imagining the crispy yet soft dough, fresh tomato sauce, and gooey mozzarella makes my mouth water. Then, there’s the legendary pasta in Bologna, home to dishes like tagliatelle al ragù, made with the freshest ingredients and rich, slow-cooked flavors. Nothing beats real Italian pasta! Beyond the food, I’d love to experience the Venetian Carnival. Walking through the streets of Venice, surrounded by people in elegant masks and elaborate costumes, with music and performances filling the air, would feel like stepping into a historical fantasy. It’s a celebration of artistry, mystery, and culture that I’d love to be a part of! Italy is a perfect blend of delicious flavors, deep-rooted traditions, and magical moments, and I can’t wait to immerse myself in it all!
