Welcome to France!

          France has always been on my travel bucket list. It’s a country filled with romance, history, art, and incredible food. Of course, the first thing that comes to mind is Paris, the City of Lights, where I can see the Eiffel Tower, walk along the Seine River, and explore the Louvre Museum. But France is more than just Paris. I want to experience the charm of Provence, the stunning beaches of the French Riviera, and the fairytale-like castles in Loire Valley.

TRAVEL

          Traveling to France from Manila is an exciting journey that requires a bit of planning. Since there are no direct flights, the best way to get there is by booking a flight with one or two layovers, usually in cities like Dubai, Doha, Istanbul, or Singapore. Most international flights land at Charles de Gaulle Airport in Paris, which is the perfect starting point for exploring the country.

          Once I arrive, getting around is easy. France has an efficient train system, making it simple to travel from Paris to places like Nice, Lyon, or Bordeaux.

LANDMARKS

          There are a lot of amazing places to visit in France, but the ones I’m most excited about are truly special. First on my list is the Eiffel Tower, not just for its breathtaking views but because I dream of proposing to my soon-to-be fiancée right there in the heart of the City of Love. It’s one of the most romantic spots in the world, and I can already picture the unforgettable moment.

          Next is the Louvre Museum, home to the famous Mona Lisa and countless other masterpieces. Walking through its grand halls and seeing all that history and art up close would be an incredible experience.

          I also want to visit the Palace of Versailles, a symbol of luxury and elegance. The stunning gardens, golden halls, and the famous Hall of Mirrors make it one of the most beautiful places in France. Exploring this palace would feel like stepping back in time to an era of kings and queens.

          France is full of history, romance, and beauty, and I can’t wait to experience it all!

EXPERIENCE

          One of the most magical experiences I dream of in France is strolling along the Seine River, taking in the breathtaking view of the Eiffel Tower and feeling the romance of Paris all around me. And when the moment feels just right, I’d love to propose right there, under the twinkling lights, making it a memory to last a lifetime.

          When it comes to food, France is a paradise. I can’t wait to try a fresh croissant from a Parisian bakery, enjoy a baguette with cheese, and have an authentic French onion soup. A proper French meal wouldn’t be complete without coq au vin, a delicious dish of chicken slow-cooked in wine, or boeuf bourguignon, a rich and hearty beef stew. And for dessert, I’d love to indulge in crème brûlée, delicate macarons, and tarte Tatin. France is all about romance, beauty, and incredible flavors, and I can’t wait to experience it all!
